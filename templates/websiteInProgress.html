Findings

Our analysis of production-related waste management in the petroleum industry from 2017 to 2022 reveals a strong commitment to environmental protection, with recycling and energy recovery as the cornerstone practices. Several key points emerge:

  • A significant dip in waste management activities in 2020, due to COVID.
  • Texas manages the vast majority of their waste, primarily through recovery techniques, treatment, and recycling.
  • Texas, California, Pennsylvania, and Louisiana are key states for the petroleum industry, each producing vast amounts of emissions compared to other states.
